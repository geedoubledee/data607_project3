The Opportunity
The Senior People Data Analyst will contribute to the People Planning & Analytics organizations’ success by providing pro-active analysis around People data, working with People Team and other Business stakeholders to identify patterns and provide actionable recommendations. As part of the organization, you’ll be working actively on budgeting activities and process and progressive improvement of the tools and processes of the team. This role collaborates closely with Finance and People team stakeholders to ensure activities are aligned with Agoda’s strategic goals and Personnel budget. The rapid growth of the business and the increasing complexity driven by new launches and services make this an exciting, and challenging proposition.
In this Role you'll get, 
Add immediate value to the organization by delivering insightful analysis, dashboards and presentations to business & leadership about People data (recruitment, attrition, performance) to support long-term strategic decision making
Get involved in the operational Budgeting and Workforce planning of the company
Provide financial analysis & commentary on month-end financials & KPIs/OKRs
Be a trusted advisor to Business Partners across the organization
Enhance the understanding and key levers of the Agoda business 
What You'll need to succeed
Bachelor’s degree required
3-5 years of experience as Data analyst, preferably in an HR environment
2-3 years of practical experience with MSSQL (or equivalent database), Tableau visualization
High level of discretion, confidentiality and ethics
Ability to work well with employees in a highly multi-cultural environment
Strong attention to detail and accuracy
Self-starter with ability to work independently, while also collaborating with cross-functional teams
Ownership mindset and ability to meet tight deadlines with a logical, methodical approach to prioritizing tasks and responsibilities
Great if you have
Experience with OneStream software
Workforce planning and Budgeting experience
Commercial awareness, ability to think both analytically and strategically, to identify about underlying business issues
Willingness to challenge the status quo and propose improvements in existing processes
Curiosity and willingness to get involved in the business to understand the story behind the numbers
