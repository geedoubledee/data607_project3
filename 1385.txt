
Genies is Gen Z’s leading avatar technology company empowering humans to create their own avatar ecosystems. Genies provides tools that allow users to create their own avatars, avatar wearable fashion lines, avatar worlds, and avatar interactive experiences in web3. The company has 99% celebrity avatar market share through its partnerships with Universal Music Group and Warner Music Group as their “official avatar and digital goods provider with thousands of Genie creators including Justin Bieber, Migos, Cardi B, and J Balvin. Our avatar marketplace “The Warehouse” built with Dapper Labs, allows creators to design and sell their avatar ecosystem creations (i.e. avatar fashion lines) to the masses. Genies has raised $200M from investors such as Silver Lake, BOND, NEA, and Breyer Capital. The Board of Directors consists of Bob Iger, Mary Meeker, Rick Yang, and Akash Nigam.
Seated in our Los Angeles based headquarters, Genies is looking to bring on our first  Product Analyst to support our growing product and business teams. Reporting to our COO & Co-Founder, our ideal candidate has created and introduced data-driven insights and analytics that support a 0 to 1 product suite and a business/revenue model as it scales. You will lead critical cross-functional projects and partner with key product & business stakeholders to empower teams with data and insights to make informed decisions to improve growth, engagement, and retention of Genies rapidly growing user-base. As a key product and business collaborator, you’ll lead the efforts in identifying the right tools and features that allow our users to integrate intelligent capabilities into their avatar ecosystems for further utility. If you’re passionate about the future of Social Networking, and empowering consumers to own their own avatar ecosystems, then Genies is your next experience!
