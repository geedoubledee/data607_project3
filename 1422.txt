Job Overview:

The Data and Technology team is responsible for discovering Big Data insights and identifying business opportunities through the use of statistical, algorithmic, mining and visualization techniques. We are supporting our internal customers by coming up with cutting-edge algorithms to support daily business.

As a data scientist in the team you will…

Integrate and prepare large, varied datasets, using specialized databases and computing environments
Turn data into critical information and knowledge that can be used to make sound organizational decisions
Be a creative thinker and propose innovative ways to look at problems by using data approaches on the set of

information available

Need to validate your findings using an experimental and iterative approach
P erform data acquisition, data preparation, data cleansing, data modeling, analysis and visualization
Work closely with our project managers, business units, data analysts and other IT teams
Use best practices in data analytics
Work with the team to help develop and automate the models
Use and share new ways of analysis within the team
Keep on top of the latest tread in the industry to research and apply new analytical methods

Qualifications

Qualifications:

Master’s degree with a very strong academic background in statistics (e.g. mathematics, statistics, computer science, data mining, machine learning, operations research, physics or a related quantitative discipline)
Doctoral Degree or PhD advantageous
3+ (with Master’s degree) years of experience in advanced analytics data analysis. Experience on the whole life cycle of model building for solving real-world complex problems. Preferable some expereince within the automotive or mobiliy industry
A deep understanding of statistical and predictive modeling concepts, including machine-learning or data mining approaches
Strong statistical modelling experience and data aggregation skills
An expert in analyzing large, complex, multi-dimensional data sets.
Data visualization knowledge
Familiarity with cloud ecosystem (Azure Cloud, Hive, Spark, Kafka)
Exceptionnally good communication skills towards colleagues and management
Excellent written and communications skills to report back the findings in a clear, structured manner are required
Experience in working in an agile team with internal and external ressources
Proficient in at least one common data analysis tool such as Anaconda or R Studio (Anaconda preferred)
Proficient knowledge in SQL, Python and R (knowledge in Pyspark, SparkR or Scala is a plus), proficient in data visualization (i.e. proficient in Tableau or PowerBI)
Familiarity with modern software development practices (Git, JIRA, CI/CD methods, MLOps)
Fluent in English
