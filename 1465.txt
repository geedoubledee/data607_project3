The platform team is responsible for executing the vision and achieving the goals of our three core lines of business: Indexing, Digital Distribution, and Data & Analytics. Made up of developers, data scientists, designers, and project managers, the platform team is the engine that drives forward the technical implementation of VettaFi’s offerings.

The opportunity is grounded in and powered by the impressive digital footprint that VettaFi has, including web domains like ETFTrends.com, ETFdb.com, AdvisorPerspectives.com, and VettaFi.com, and will impact the millions of investors that these domains attract on a consistent basis. The role will be responsible for driving technical excellence in data engineering, statistical techniques, and domain specific applications.

· Lead the technical direction of the data science team

· Review and approve pull requests

Who are WE:

VettaFi is a high-growth and profitable technology company that sits at the nexus of three exciting mega trends:

· Business intelligence (including the application of machine learning)

· The evolution of financial services, both through innovations in product and product delivery

· The internet as a medium

Through its wholly owned and operated brands, collectively the most influential digital voice in the world of exchange traded funds (ETFs) -- ETF Database and ETF Trends -- VettaFi interacts with tens of thousands of US financial advisors at an unparalleled level of depth every week.

With an educational mission, VettaFi produces and disseminates numerous types of multimedia content to fuel the research process of financial advisors, including webcasts, video, native content channels, interactive data-driven tools, and editorial content.
