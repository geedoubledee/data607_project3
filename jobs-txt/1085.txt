About the job
Title: Data Scientist

Location: San Francisco, CA or Remote

Salary: $100,000 - 150,000

Contact: Paul Chatlos, pchatlos@smithhanley.com


Venture capital fund based in the Bay Area is seeking to hire their first Data Scientist to assist them in their investment portfolio decisions


Data Scientist Overview


As part of an established venture capital fund with significant access to capital in the Menlo Park, CA area, this Data Scientist position will work within a lean team to conduct exceptional financial diligence, source new opportunities, and provide portfolio company support in the Internet, Media and Fin Tech Sectors. The team invests across stages from pre-seed to pre-IPO and takes an opportunistic approach to investments. This individual will be based in the Bay Area and compensation will be commensurate with experience.


Data Scientist Primary responsibilities include


-Create and refine models based on historical startup success and scoring system for sourcing purpose

-Regularly screen companies within DST's database to surface companies with key developments

-Develop investment thesis using quantitative research

-Facilitate portfolio management by connecting API into portfolio data sources

-Create and maintain database of companies evaluated by investment team as well as track performance of investment decision

-Effectively communicate with an ability to interpret and present analytical insights to stakeholders

-Undergraduate degree. B.S. Computer Science, Data Science or Statistics from top tier University

-1-3 years of previous data scientist experience. A plus if within Venture Capital, Finance or investing space

-Data science/engineering and computer programming project experience

-Data science skills including Python, SQL, regression and statistical modeling

-Basic understanding of ML/AI and familiarity of key frameworks such as Tree Model, CNN, TensorFlow and Scikit-Learn

-Basic front-end and back-end programing skills such as Javascript, HTML and PHP

-Exceptional technical, analytical and research skills

-Familiar with Excel VBA and PowerPoint

-Experience with database such as AWS and MongoDB

-Familiar with data visualization tools such as PowerBI and Tableau

-Data engineering and web-scraping skills

-Familiar with PHP programming

-Familiar with services providers such as Crunchbase, Tracxn, Pitchbook, SecondMeasure, etc.

-Strong attention to detail

-Hard working
