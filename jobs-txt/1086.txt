Work alongside Enterprise Data Architecture team members to ensure data is collected, cleansed, and transformed to efficiently bring your algorithms to life
Establish and develop experimentation frameworks to foster clear, easy to understand statistical outputs that will drive iterative process improvement
Present data and solutions effectively across geographically dispersed teams from a diverse set of technical backgrounds, including leadership and executive teams
Complete assigned tickets including functional/technical development, testing, review, UAT, and deployment while communicating progress and blockers
Promote best practices and help guide the broader team toward continuous improvement
Understand data science/ML trends and the practical application of existing and emerging technologies to enable new and evolving business needs
Recommend appropriate tools or technologies to leverage across advanced analytics use cases
Communicate complex solutions clearly and effectively to all levels of the organization
Strategically partner with Product to inform feature development, demonstrating what will drive business value
