About the job
Job Title: Data Scientist


Job Description:


We are seeking a talented Data Scientist to join a HealthTech Firm based in Florida. The ideal candidate will be responsible for developing and implementing algorithms and statistical models to analyze large datasets, and communicate insights to stakeholders.


Responsibilities:


Use advanced statistical analysis and machine learning techniques to analyze large datasets and identify patterns, trends and insights.
Develop and implement predictive models to optimize business performance and outcomes.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify business problems and develop solutions.
Communicate results and insights to stakeholders through data visualizations and presentations.
Continuously monitor and improve data quality, accuracy, and consistency.
Stay current with latest developments in machine learning, data science, and technology trends.
Requirements:

Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Statistics, or related field.
3+ years of experience in data analysis and modeling using tools such as Python, R, and SQL.
Strong understanding of statistical modeling techniques such as regression, clustering, decision trees, and neural networks.
Experience with machine learning algorithms such as Random Forest, SVM, Neural Networks, and Gradient Boosting.
Proficient in data visualization tools such as Tableau, Power BI, or D3.js.
Strong problem-solving skills and ability to communicate complex findings in simple terms.
Experience working with large datasets and distributed computing frameworks such as Hadoop, Spark, or AWS EMR.
Strong understanding of data structures, data modeling, and database design.
Experience working with unstructured data such as text, images, and videos is a plus.
Strong programming skills in Python, R, or other languages commonly used in data analysis.

If you have the required skills and experience, and are excited about working in a fast-paced, dynamic environment, we encourage you to apply for this exciting opportunity.
