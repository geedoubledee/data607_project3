Bardess is looking for a skilled data scientist.

Bardess is a data analytics consultancy which specializes in data science, data architecture/engineering, and BI. Our data science clients span all industries, including technology, life sciences, financial services, CPG, retail, media, and manufacturing.

You will be working alongside highly experienced technical consultants, several of whom have PhDs in computational scientific fields, as well as project managers and sales staff. Bardess consultants are self-motivated, results-oriented people, and diplomatic problem solvers.

Recent example projects

Transaction clustering on 2 billion rows of point-of-sale data, using Spark - for major convenience store chain
Credit risk model for major vehicle leasing company
Store/product revenue predictions, sales forecasting, and optimization for major retail company
Business partnership with new time series forecasting software product
