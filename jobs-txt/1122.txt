About the job
We have been engaged by a well respected, publicly traded financial institution in Northern California to identify a Data Scientist who will be critical in making contributions to their increased investment into Risk management, Fraud detection, Customer experience and Algorithmic trading.


This is a hybrid role (2-3 days a week on-site) and can sit in either Novato, CA or Sacramento, CA


Job Description:

The Data Scientist oversees the collection, storage and interpretation of data and works closely with business stakeholders to understand goals and determine how data can be used to achieve those goals. Designs data modeling processes, create algorithms and predictive models to extract the data needed and analyze the data and share insights with peers.


Essential Job Functions:

Collect data by analyzing business results or by performing and managing new studies
Brainstorm innovative ways to use our unique data to answer business problems and measure success against targets.
Extract and understand data from internals core systems and external manual inputs to derive relevant insights and executive product health dashboards
Transfer data into format appropriate for analysis
Create new, experimental frameworks to collect data
Build tools to automate data collection
Perform data mining for usable information to understand industry tends as well as current and anticipated customer needs
Create reports and presentations for business use
Develop visualizations to make complex analyses accessible to a broad audience
Correlate similar data to find actionable results and influence product team through presentation of data-based recommendations
Work with stakeholders throughout the organization to identify opportunities for leveraging core data to drive business growth
Engage in productive discussions with external or internal stakeholders and synthesize ideas
Mine and analyze data from company databases to drive optimization and improvement of product, marketing techniques and business strategies for the Bank and its clients
Assess the effectiveness and accuracy of new data sources and data gathering techniques
Develop custom data models and algorithms to apply to data sets
Use predictive modeling to increase and optimize customer experiences, revenue generation, data insights, advertising targeting and other business outcomes
Develop processes and tools to monitor and analyze model performance and data accuracy
Provide guidance in modern analytic techniques and business applications to unlock the value of core’s unique data set, in keeping with market trends, client needs and emerging techniques
Organize and manage multiple data science projects with diverse cross-functional stakeholders
