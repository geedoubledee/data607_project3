ROLE OVERVIEW

As a Data Scientist, you are a creative problem-solver by trade and have outstanding organizational skills, able to manage multiple responsibilities and deliverables simultaneously.


Responsible for planning, developing, and executing analytical methods for a leading brand with large analytical databases, you have a solid track record in applied analytic modeling, machine learning and algorithms, and statistical analyses.



This role will work with large, complex data sets to derive measurable business insights using machine learning and advanced analytics.




Responsibilities:

Work closely with cross-functional team members, provide delivery of analytics services with an emphasis on promotion, retail, shopper, licensing, and digital channels, including:
Predictive modeling
Machine learning
Segmentation
Time-series forecasting
Build dashboards and visualizations of processed data
Communicate insights or issues uncovered through working with the data
Proactively identify potential data improvements and augmentations from external data provided
Use predictive analytics and machine learning to drive business decisions
Support the development of measurement strategies, identifying key insights and metrics to track and report on
Deliver hands-on strategic modeling activities such as customer segmentation, targeting strategies, lifetime value, in-market timing, loyalty, response/engagement optimization, machine learning and quantitative support for ROI analyses and other modeling activities
Collaborate with client service and technical teams, and potentially directly with clients, to identify modeling goals
Track and report on our initiatives, successes, and lessons learned, and package outcomes in a client facing presentation
Prioritize tasks and work assignments, ensuring timelines and commitments are met
Participate in ad-hoc special projects as required, and other tasks as assigned
Manipulate, cleanse and perform statistical analyses
