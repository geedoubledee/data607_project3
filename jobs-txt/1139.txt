About the job
You will be helping to build a forecasting and algorithmic trading platform:

Build machine learning models to forecast demand, market prices, and participant behavior.
Build decision-making "agents" to act autonomously in power markets
Bring an experimental mindset, prototyping and developing with the right model for the job.

Requirements:

You are fluent in Python and its wider numerical ecosystem (Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn, etc.)
You have a passion for time-series forecasting and a desire to apply that within the energy sector
You have experience building algorithmic decision tools (e.g. mixed-integer linear programming)
You have exposure to energy markets or battery systems modelling
You have experience applying the data science toolset to time-series problems.
You have at least 3 years of work/educational experience developing and productionising real-time projects in Machine Learning and Data Science
You have a BA/BSc degree in Computer Science, Machine Learning or related technical field

Preferred:

MS or PhD degree in Computer Science, Machine Learning, or related technical field
Knowledge in probabilistic programming, Bayesian methods, causal inference, dynamical systems, and/or reinforcement learning
Knowledge of relational and/or non-relational databases (eg, SQL), and SQLAlchemy
Full-stack / machine learning engineering experience
Version control code (Git) including dev, test and production environments
AWS, continuous integration, monitoring and alerting
