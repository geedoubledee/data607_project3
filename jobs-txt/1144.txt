About the job
AWM Smart Shelf is reinventing retail and looking for smart, talented people to work on building exciting applications. The initial focus of this position is working on AWM Frictionless which is a cashierless-shopping solution for shoppers to be able to grab what they want from a store and simply walk out.

Our solution uses real-time computer vision and deep learning to determine what shoppers take. We're looking for an experienced Data Scientist to help analyze, improve, and further develop our predictive algorithms.

We’re looking for nimble, practical problem solvers and we value a diversity of perspectives. If this sounds like fun, we'd love to hear from you!
