About the job
605 is an independent TV measurement and analytics firm that offers advertising and content measurement, full-funnel attribution, media planning, optimization and analytical solutions. Comprised of engineers, analysts, data scientists, media experts and marketing strategists, 605 forges new paths using groundbreaking innovations that set industry standards for audience targeting and measurement.

Senior Data Scientists at 605 are highly skilled data scientists, statisticians, and/or other skilled researchers (PhD, ABD, Masters with experience, or comparable). The Senior Data Scientist must be able to analyze big data, apply machine learning and statistical models, and communicate findings clearly to all levels of an organization.

Responsibilities include:
Coding original R/Python/Spark scripts as well as optimizing current scripts daily
Implementing machine learning and statistical models to solve complex problems
Flexibly working on a variety of different externally facing client projects over the course of a week
Collaborating with fellow data scientists, data engineers and account managers to identify and implement efficiencies and automate solutions
Mentoring junior data scientists and analysts
Working with sales leads to build new business opportunities

Requirements
7+ years work experience in comparable field and a Master's degree (or equivalent work experience)
5+ years coding experience with 2+ years specifically in R or Python
3+ years implementing machine learning and statistical methods to solve complex problems
Strong machine learning and/or statistical skills and intuition. Expertise in at least one of: causality, model evaluation, or forecasting
Experience adapting, implementing, and scaling various solutions utilizing machine learning techniques
Experience manipulating multiple big data sets
Adaptable enough to use existing tools, models, and methods to new needs
Strict integrity working with confidential, private, and high-value client information
Ability to balance workload across a variety of different projects over the course of a week
Excellent attention to detail
Excellent oral and written communication skills to interpret and translate results to a variety of technical and non-technical audiences through reports; white papers; and internal, external, and client-facing presentations

Preferred Skills
Previous experience with Spark
Experience working in data science in the television or digital marketing industry
Experience designing and analyzing experiments
Experience analyzing high-dimensional time series or event stream data
