
Company DescriptionPrivia Health™ is a technology-driven, national physician enablement company that collaborates with medical groups, health plans, and health systems to optimize physician practices, improve patient experiences, and reward doctors for delivering high-value care in both in-person and virtual settings. The Privia Platform is led by top industry talent and exceptional physician leadership, and consists of scalable operations and end-to-end, cloud-based technology that reduces unnecessary healthcare costs, achieves better outcomes, and improves the health of patients and the well-being of providers.Job DescriptionAs Privia Health continues to grow, there is an increased need to more efficiently manage new payer data as well as analyze data quality from legacy relationships. Under the supervision of team management, a Payer Data Analyst will aid or lead in the acquisition, management, analysis, and interpretation of the data Privia receives from various payer sources. These sources include, but are not limited to: membership/eligibility, attribution, medical claims, pharmacy claims, and VBC or quality gap data feeds. A Payer Data Analyst will transform data into accurate, consistent and timely information that can be readily communicated to all relevant stakeholders. They will be a part of a team striving to ensure and improve data quality, data governance, and payer data management across the enterprise.Essential Job Duties:Aid in the acquisition and monitoring of all relevant payer data. Be able to efficiently write SQL queries as requested for ad hoc insight when answers are unknown. Communicate data needs and define specifications for data management team's support for standardizing data components to support scalable, automated and productized impact reports.For new value base contracts and health plans, participate and help lead in the negotiations for data alongside the Payer Relations team by outlining data needs and specifications to ensure payer accountability.Partner with internal product and engineering teams to execute data cleansing strategies to aid the use of payer data to support Privia Health’s mission.QualificationsBachelor's Degree Preferred.4+ years job experience in a Healthcare Data, Analytics, or related field.Strong qualitative and quantitative skills, with emphasis on effectively communicating complex data to a varied audience using methods to ensure comprehension.Proven ability to analytically problem solve in a dynamic and complex environment.Working knowledge to proficient in SQL/query languages.Advanced experience with Microsoft Excel or other spreadsheet software.Ability to prioritize and manage concurrent projects while showing strong organizational skills that will allow for management of payer data across multiple markets and lines of business.Execute work with exceptional attention to detail on all project tasks.Ability to work independently and within a diverse team environment.Ability to learn quickly, think independently, and develop new processes/analyses.Ability to prioritize tasks with little supervision, work under time pressure, and excel with time management skills. Qualifications (Preferred)Previous experience working with large datasets.
