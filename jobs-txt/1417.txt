Position Overview

The Data Scientist is responsible for using advanced analytic methods to extract value from business data and influence teams and decisions with data-based recommendations. The Data Scientist is responsible for modeling complex business problems and discovering business insights through the use of statistical, algorithmic, mining, and visualization tools. Additionally, the Data Scientist integrates and prepares large, varied datasets, architecting specialized database and computing environments, and communicating results.

The Data Scientist provides data that is congruent and reliable, proposes innovative ways to look at problems using data mining approaches, validates findings using experimental and iterative approaches, and presents back his/her findings by exposing assumptions and validations in a way that can be easily understood by business stakeholders.

Essential Job Functions

Designs experiments, tests hypotheses, and builds models
Conducts advanced data analysis and complex designs algorithm
Applies statistical and predictive modeling techniques to build, maintain, and improve on multiple real-time decision systems
Models and frames business scenarios that are meaningful and have an impact on critical business processes and/or decisions
Works with IT teams to support data collection, integration, and retention requirements based on the input collected with the business
Develops experimental design approaches to validate finding or test hypotheses
Employs the appropriate algorithm to discover patterns
Educates the organization both from IT and the business perspectives on new approaches, such as testing hypotheses and statistical validation of results
Inspires business stakeholders about the art of the possible, for example, by citing use cases from industry peers
Solicits project ideas from different business stakeholders
Creates a range of implementation scenarios from build, buy versus outsource and understands the corresponding cost, risk and benefit components
Catalogs, documents and manages different datasets, internal or external
Appropriately vectorizes (transform, convert and model) text, image, audio, machine/consumer data, time-series data and potentially geolocation or log data
Performs data sampling and data aggregation, fuzzy encoding, etc.
Generates smart hypotheses of the underlying mechanics of the business process
Applies a wide array of machine learning models: various regression and clustering techniques, ensemble techniques (random forests, XGBoost) and time-series analysis
Performs real world testing of machine learning (ML) models such as champion-challenger (A/B testing) and cross-validation
Continuously monitors the execution and health of models within the process environment
Performs other duties as assigned
