Work you will do:
As a Data Scientist, you will be responsible for all phases of Data Science life cycle development, including data discovery, labeling the data, working with tech team(s) to create hypothesis, assist in selecting models, test and evaluate models and model operationalization. You will adhere to corporate reporting policy and controls to ensure the accuracy, timeliness, and confidentiality of Data Management automation. The ideal candidate must be able to drive real business value by collaborating with Data Managers and Clinical Data Scientists under the direction of the Head of AI and Data Sciences.

Job Responsibilities:
• Run SQL queries to extract operational, clinical data, and specifications from various information systems.
• Independently assess and analyze data quality, understand business processes, and how they impact data quality, prepare data including labeling of data for Machine Learning experiments.
• Pilot and build POCs to demonstrate and test the business value of Machine Learning models, understand Model metrics, iteratively improve Model performance working with tech leads and vendors.
• Analyze, interpret, and summarize Machine Learning model results.
• Operationalize and maintain the models.
• Develop training materials for business users to increase their understanding of AI, Data Science, and automation within Data Management.
• Find opportunities for model and process optimization for various business scenarios.
• Develop testing frameworks and test model quality.
• Support AI and Data Science Leads to coordinate with different data management/functional teams to implement models and monitor outcomes.
• Support the development of processes and Best Practices to monitor and analyze model performance and accuracy.
