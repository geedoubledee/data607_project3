About the job

About the Role:

Seerist is looking for a Data Scientist to join our Data Science team in the Americas region. We are a small team where you will have autonomy to create, train, test, and deploy new models on a regular basis. We are looking for individuals capable of working independently while also participating in collaborative exchange of ideas.

Responsibilities:

Define model objectives, constraints, and metrics with customers
Estimate project timelines
Identify and evaluate training and prediction data sources
Prepare and clean data
Perform feature engineering on models
Select model type
Train and evaluate models
Deploy models
Assist in model result visualization
Explain model results
Stay current with academic research and commercial developments in the machine learning field

Required Qualifications:

Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science or related fields
At least 2 years of professional experience with machine learning, neural networks / deep learning
Experience with software development in python
Ability to work independently and collaboratively
Excellent verbal/written communication and organization skills

Desired Qualifications:

Master’s or PhD Degree preferred
Experience in any of the following:
natural language processing / computational linguistics
mathematical modeling
statistics and forecasting
geospatial analysis
data pipeline engineering
US Citizenship to work with some government customers
