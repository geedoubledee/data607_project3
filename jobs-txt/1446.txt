Machine Learning Engineer

Remote (EST Time Zone)

Contract-to-Hire

Brooksource is looking for several Software Engineers to join their Fortune 18 Retail client's Machine Learning Enablement & Team. As a ML Engineer you will analyze how internal users have adopted ML tools in the environment, identify their needs, and work with Custom Tools team to strategically plan out how to make machine learning experience in the company better. The goal is to help internal customers improve and streamline their machine learning projects/applications.

Requirements
Basic understanding and interest towards machine learning and data science
Experience in common programming language, prefer but not limit to python
Understanding of relational database and SQL
Ability to work with customers, take requirements and solve business issues
Analytics experience to aggregate data and visualize as needed
Additional bonus technologies: GCP, Google Vortex, Sci Kit, Panda, Numpy, Tenser Flow, Spark, ML Flow
Responsibilities
Pair program daily with a Senior Engineer
Work closely with internal customers – ranging from BAs to Data Scientists – to understand their business issues
Resolve customer business issues
Ability to advocate for ML tool that is being implemented streamline
Ability to write code to bridge the gap between homegrown application and third party software
