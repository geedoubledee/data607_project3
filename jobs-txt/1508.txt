Data Science Manager
Description
The Data Science Manager is responsible for leading three teams across Alabama Power Company (APC) and Southern Company Services (SCS); including, APC Predictive Analytics, SCS Marketing Analytics, and Energy Information eXchange (EIX).
This position manages direct reports and multiple consultants/contractors including data scientists, data engineers, and product managers across several functional areas and geographies.
The Data Science Manager is responsible for leading the APC Predictive Analytics team in creating, analyzing, and enhancing predictive and analytical models using both structured and unstructured data to inform growth decisions and strategies across APC Marketing and Economic Development and Customer Services. Additionally, this position is responsible for leading the SCS Marketing Analytics team in the development and enhancement of analytical models in support of marketing, sales, and development efforts across multiple operating companies and the Southern Company system. Lastly, this position leads the development, enhancement, and execution of the Southern Company Energy Information eXchange (EIX), which is Southern Company’s cloud-based data and analytics enablement platform.
The Data Science Manager will lead the creation of analytics and communicate the strategic value and actionable insights from complex predictive modeling leveraging data science techniques with a focus on all customer classes (regulated and unregulated). This role will lead the development and usage of qualitative and quantitative customer data from internal, external, public, and purchased data.
The Data Science Manager leads Southern Company system engagements for strategic partnerships and ventures as the subject matter expert for marketing and customer data and analytics. Additionally, this role bridges cross-organizational teams to underpin the business strategy and key initiatives with relevant data and analytics. Further, this role will serve as a strategic partner in Southern Company’s efforts to modernize our data architecture and analytics platforms to enable the company to meet our customers’ needs today and in the future. Similarly, this role will participate in the development of Southern Company’s customer data strategy.
MAJOR JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:
Identify opportunities, drive business profitability, increase efficiencies, and create greater customer awareness
Lead APC and SCS teams in creating, enhancing, and deploying statistical and analytical models using both structured and unstructured data to inform growth decisions and strategies across APC Marketing and Economic Development and system marketing, sales, and development efforts
Lead the development, enhancement, and execution of Southern Company EIX cloud-based data and analytics enablement platform
Lead contractor, vendor, and consultant partners and allies that support the development and execution of APC and SCS analytics strategy
Convey data driven insights for growth decisions and strategies across the residential, commercial, industrial, and unregulated segments
Develop deeper understanding of our customers via internal and external data
Target growth sectors and opportunities through advanced, repeatable, and deployable predictive models
Manage multiple, concurrent projects with competing deadlines
Manage and improve existing data sets and analytical tools
Support the organization’s access to, understanding of, and engagement with internal and external data sources and systems
Communicate technical information and complex data to non-technical audiences in a logical and concise manner
Ensure the integrity of data being used through cleansing and maintenance
Discover and recommend new data sources to complement existing data
Ensure teams and clients know what data is available to them
Lead the development of tools for clients to leverage within their roles
Lead and actively participate in cross-functional and cross-system data and analytics focused efforts to identify and successfully execute future growth opportunities
