What People Analytics & Insights contributes to Cardinal Health
The People and Analytics Insights (PAI) team provides insights that drive outcomes. Our mission is to empower all leaders and HR stakeholders within Cardinal Health to make data-driven people strategy decisions quickly, easily, and with confidence.
The Data & Analytics Function oversees the analytics life cycle in order to identify, analyze and present relevant insights that drive business decisions and anticipate opportunities to achieve a competitive advantage. This function manages analytic data platforms, the access, design and implementation of reporting/business intelligence solutions, and the application of advanced quantitative modeling.
Job Summary
As Manager of the People Data Science team, you are responsible for the strategic execution and management of the data product portfolio. You will drive consistency and scalability throughout team consulting engagements and projects, often leveraging RStudio Connect to deliver automated and interactive dashboards and applications. Alongside the team, you will help translate business needs into insights through advanced statistical solutions and models. You will also partner closely with the People Analytics Consulting team to support HR and the business to answer key questions related to people and talent strategy.
Responsibilities
Engage with HR and business stakeholders to ensure the design, build, and delivery of insights are aligned with overall HR and team strategic direction
Manage a team of data science professionals, developing both consulting and technical (analytical) competencies
Emphasize a continuous improvement mindset to balance scalability and innovation within the data product portfolio, data workflows and processes
Ability to clearly communicate complex data and insights to a variety of audiences and develop actionable recommendations
Maintain best practices in data science and data management across the analytics life cycle, aligning PAI and other Cardinal Health analytics team
