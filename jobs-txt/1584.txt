Overview:
The position
We are seeking a Machine Learning Engineer to join our Communities Organization to help A Place for Mom to achieve its ambitious goals by deriving insights from our data. The Machine Learning Engineer will support the team by creating and productionalizing machine learning models to answer business questions and to solve business problems. The Machine Learning Engineer will closely work with senior leadership in a high performing team environment and will play a pivotal role in growing A Place for Mom’s business.
Responsibilities:
What you will do
Work and collaborate with stakeholders throughout the organization to identify opportunities for data science solutions
Implement models, analyze models’ performance, and monitor business outcomes (customer experience, revenue, etc.)
Integrate models into a framework and enable API access
Implement iteratively improving, end-to-end machine learning pipelines: from business question, to data, to training, to API
Enhance company’s machine learning framework, debug and correct data assumption through machine learning
Mine and analyze data from large datasets to improve business processes and strategies
Assess the effectiveness and accuracy of new data sources, develop efficient data gathering techniques
Assess the methodological and functional pros and cons of implementing third-party products versus building an in-house system when facing a business problem.
Qualifications:
Required skills and competencies
A Bachelor’s degree in at least one science, technology, engineering, demography, economics, statistics or mathematics field; or an equivalent related professional experience
At least 3 years’ experience manipulating data sets and building statistical or machine learning models
Experience with large data sets and proficiency with SQL and Python, PySpark experience is a plus
Knowledge of a variety of machine learning techniques (clustering, decision trees, time series, artificial neural networks, etc.) and their real-world advantages/drawbacks
Knowledge and experience with statistical techniques (regression, distributions, hypothesis testing, etc.)
Strong analytical skills, including the ability to break down business scenarios into manageable components
Experience in Google Analytics or Adobe Analytics is a plus
Excellent written and verbal communication skills to coordinate across teams and to tell stories with data
A drive to learn and master new technologies and techniques, curiosity and passion for making an impact
