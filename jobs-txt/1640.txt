DIRECTOR OF DATA ENGINEERING
The Director of Data Engineering will be leading teams to deliver new and enhanced data products that are highly performant and scalable to drive consumption of data from a variety of sources, while driving data engineering best practices. This leader must have the ability to work on competing priorities and have the technical skills to ensure that we are building compelling and stable data solutions. The Director will work on continuously improving the technical excellence by hiring and developing talent to create a high performing team.
ESSENTIAL DUTIES:
Direct and oversee the build and operation of reliable data engineering services leveraging a range of on-prem and cloud technologies such as SQL Server, Azure Data Factory, Azure Data Lake, Azure Synapse, Databricks and Event Hubs
Set and maintain appropriate security controls for data engineering team and user groups in accordance with business, security and data privacy requirements
Set and maintain technical design and build standards and best practices for integrations and data engineering services including but not limited to – security, privacy/compliance, development of data pipelines, configuring cloud services to optimize costs, error handling and support management
Oversee the implementation and maintenance of monitoring & fault handling system to ensure SLAs are met for data refreshes and/or updates
Oversee the design, build and support of operational data stores and APIs that can be consumed by web and/or mobile apps
Partner with other Platform Directors in the organization and formulate innovative strategies and techniques for integrations, data modeling and other data engineering services
Partner with Architecture to enhance and maintain integrations and data engineering tool kits and tool selection guidelines
Manage, mentor, and grow the integrations and data engineering staff to support the growing needs of the organization
QUALIFICATIONS
EDUCATION/CERTIFICATIONS:
Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, Data Science or equivalent experience is required
Microsoft Azure certifications are preferred
TECHNICAL/SOFT SKILLS:
Strong experience in design and software technical architecture for building large scale Data Analytics
Strong knowledge with Modern Data platforms, Data design and Data integration tools is required
Experience enhancing customer and employee experiences by delivering services that are easy to use and cost effective
Experience with leading edge cloud technologies to build and deploy reliable and scalable products
Firm understanding of the Agile Software Development Life Cycle process
Ability to understanding the products and services offered by the Firm and synthesize the data from them for different audiences
Eloquent communication skills with technical and non-technical audience
EXPERIENCE:
10 years of experience in IT career implementing enterprise data solutions leveraging modern data technologies required
3 years of experience building data engineering services leveraging cloud (preferably Azure) required
Knowledge and experience with Data Quality, Data Modeling, Data Warehousing, Data Governance, Privacy and Security required
5 years of experience as people manager required.
Strong Experience leading, scaling, and developing diverse Data Engineering teams required
Experience mentoring teams through design, development and support activities while encouraging staff to share responsibility in problem solving required
Experience implementing one or more enterprise class solutions with SQL Server, Azure SQL, Microservices based solutions required
Experience leading the building of data services that support big data analytics and ML workflows preferred
Experience leading the building and publishing of APIs and leveraging API management technologies preferred
Solid working knowledge of agile development processes preferred
Experience using Azure DevOps preferred
LEADERSHIP SKILLS:
Strong leadership skills and the ability to work effectively with the Business and IT staff
Ability to build strong relationships across lines of business, functional areas, and outside vendors
Ability to understand and articulate business imperatives as well as business impact with the use of data for analytical and operational purposes
Ability to articulate vision and strategy of data platform with different types of audience required
Excellent verbal, written and interpersonal communication skills, including the ability to communicate effectively within IT, Firm Leadership, and the Business. Strong ability to create and lead presentations to leadership
Progressive experience leading data engineering teams
Strong learning, analytical and strategic leadership mindset
Ability to lead geographically distributed teams
Ability to resolve conflicts timely and beneficially to all concerned
