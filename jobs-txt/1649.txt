Overview:

HarperCollins is looking for a Director of Data Science to lead the Data Science team.

The Data Science team works closely with sales, inventory management, editorial, and other teams to solve complex challenges and drive business growth by developing algorithms, building automated data tools, and providing data-driven recommendations.

This role will report to the Chief Digital Officer.
Responsibilities:
Leads, manages, and mentors a small team of data scientists and analysts.
Serves as team lead on multiple projects and participates in development, validation, and delivery of algorithms and tools.
Collaborates with business stakeholders, data engineers, and software developers to understand key business questions and design and deploy analytic solutions.
Owns the full development cycle of machine learning models and data-driven tools that drive commercial impact in business areas, including marketing, pricing, demand forecasting, search engine optimization.
Collaborates with business leaders and contributes to long-term data science and business roadmap.
Gets to the core of issues concerning the business, helping team members with Python and SQL as needed.
Qualifications:
1+ years of experience managing analytic professionals
5+ years of experience in data science-related roles
Proficiency in Python, SQL, git, and common data science libraries, like pandas, scikit-learn, and tensorflow
Proficiency in statistical modeling, experimental set-up, data analysis, predictive model development, and model deployment
Understanding of best practices for leading high-functioning data teams (git, documentation, etc.)
Experience distilling stakeholder requirements into problem definitions and delivering solutions
Strong communication skills with the ability to effectively communicate with technical and non-technical stakeholders
