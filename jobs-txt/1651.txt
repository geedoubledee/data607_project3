Schneider is seeking an Engineering and Data Science Manager to join our thriving team! In this role you will manage the data science, optimization and artificial intelligence services of the assigned function or department within the organization. You'll also collaborate with your team to develop and conduct work programs in accordance with company's policies, priorities and budget.
About the role:
Development and implementation of D4 (Dynamic Data Driven Decisions) models and components utilizing technologically advanced Data Science, Optimization and AI algorithms and techniques
Lead the use of complex data science or optimization analysis for specific business problems or questions. Understand the uses and limitations of statistical and optimization methodologies
Interpret engineering results and present customer specific solutions through written and oral proposals and presentations
Direct the work of two or more associates
Maintain consistent attendance while exhibiting behavior in alignment with our core values

Bachelor degree with an emphasis in Operations Research, Industrial Engineering, Mathematics, Data Science or Statistics
Master degree with an emphasis in Operations Research, Industrial Engineering Mathematics, Data Science or Statistics is preferred

Expertise in the use of complex data science or optimization analysis
