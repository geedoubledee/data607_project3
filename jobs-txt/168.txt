
Job Description
Key Responsibilities:
Create compelling visualizations, dashboards and reports for internal and external purposes
Design and develop visualization standards to support product development based on industry best practices and current research
Create and maintain visualization galleries for marketing and promotional material
Collaborate with multidisciplinary team of designers, developers and system administrators
Compile and analyze data, processes, and codes to troubleshoot problems and identify areas for improvement
Collaborating with other team members to establish objectives and design more functional, cohesive codes to enhance the user experience
Developing ideas for new programs, products, or features by monitoring industry developments and trends
Recording data and reporting it to proper parties, such as clients or leadership
Experience and Qualifications: 
 
Experience developing dashboards and web applications in RShiny
Experience creating aesthetically appealing visualizations with R packages such as ggplot2
Experience performing data wrangling with R packages such as data.table or dplyr
Experience with UI/UX design principles
Ability to tell a story with data to a wide range of users
Strong attention to detail including clear, concise and thorough documentation skills
Ability to listen and develop a deep understanding of project, product and client requirements
Strong self-initiative and work ethic
Demonstrated ability to work well in a multi-disciplinary team environment
Ability to learn and adapt new technologies
Knowledge of accessibility standards such as WCAG or AODA
Knowledge of visualizing geographic data is considered an asset
 
Additional Experience and Qualifications an asset: 
Candidates must provide a digital portfolio outlining their abilities to perform the above stated responsibilities, experience and qualifications.
Candidates must hold or be able to obtain Security and Controlled Goods clearances
