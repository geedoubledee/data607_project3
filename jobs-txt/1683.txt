Job Description:
THE OPPORTUNITY

The Data Science Manager will lead on large transformational data science projects to capture, action, and harness the power of our data as the world’s largest publisher and distributor of children’s books. .
Reporting into the Executive Director of Data Science & Data Products, the Data Science Manager will play a central operational role in advancing the application of machine learning, statistical analysis and data science across the full range of Scholastic businesses and functions: ecommerce, digital subscriptions, direct mail, telemarketing, field sales, customer service, operations / fulfillment, and product development.
RESPONSIBILITIES
In partnership with business stakeholders across the company, frame data science initiatives to establish business strategies and solve business challenges such that the data science initiative drives actionable results.
Manage large data science projects end-to-end: problem scoping, data wrangling, analysis, implementation, presentation, performance monitoring
Lead execution of advanced machine learning, both supervised or unsupervised; mentor others in this regard
Lead data science initiatives to leverage net-new data acquisitions by Scholastic to uncover strategic insights into Scholastic’s customer base and merchandising decisions
Deploy predictive models in AWS (lambda, data pipelines, model endpoints, API gateways)
Grasp the full picture of Scholastic’s lines of business, and how their goals align with enterprise goals; contribute as a thought leader to cultivate a data culture at Scholastic through data science.
Encourage our business partners to become more comfortable with data science initiatives while advancing the analytical savviness of the use of data throughout the company
WHO WE ARE
Scholastic Corporation (NASDAQ: SCHL) is the world's largest publisher and distributor of children's books, a leading provider of core literacy curriculum and professional services, and a producer of educational and entertaining children's media. The Company creates quality books and ebooks, print and technology-based learning programs for pre-K to grade 12, classroom magazines and other products and services that support children's learning both in school and at home. With operations in 14 international offices and exports to 165 countries, Scholastic makes quality, affordable books available to all children around the world through school-based book clubs and book fairs, classroom collections, school and public libraries, retail and online. True to its mission of 100+ years to encourage the personal and intellectual growth of all children beginning with literacy, the Company has earned a reputation as a trusted partner to educators and families.
