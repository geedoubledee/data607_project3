The Business Intelligence Developer supports the strategic initiatives and tactical challenges of the credit union from a data integration and development perspective. The role partners with data analysts to gain understanding of business objectives and then configure data assets for subsequent use by analysis tools. Efforts will be focused on opportunities to gain valuable business insights related to operational efficiency, growth opportunities, and risk mitigation.
Essential Duties/Responsibilities
Write Structured Query Language (SQL) commands including Data Definition Language (DDL), Data Manipulation Language (DML), and Data Query Language (DQL).
Develop, test, and maintain data integration projects and packages within SQL Server Integration Services (SSIS).
Tune data queries for optimal performance and data storage.
Design the best methods of integrating data from several different sources and formats for use in data reporting and analysis.
Develop, test, and maintain data integration across enterprise data assets.
Develop and implement data collection systems which optimize statistical efficiency and data quality.
Import, clean, validate, and model data with the purpose of enabling decision makers to gain business insights.
Develop complex data mappings to document data flows through multiple databases to ensure accuracy
Responsible for improving and maintaining data integrity using metadata, data profiling and analysis techniques.
Serve as a liaison between data analysts, other developers, and system administrators for data-related projects and maintenance.
Responsible for the ongoing development of the enterprise data warehouse elements and sources.
Maintain and improve data governance standards in coordination with the internal Data Governance Committee.
Research developments and trends in business intelligence including new products, services and technological advances.
Research and recommend data tools appropriate for the organization.
Develop, test, and maintain reporting tools including dashboards, reports, and the related distribution to users.
Assist analysts with the identification and interpretation of trends or patterns in complex data sets.
Actively promote and contribute to the achievement of organizational goals.
Recommend, develop, and implement new policies and procedures.
Write thorough documentation of technical specifications to ensure business continuity.
Pursue ongoing education and learn/apply new skills and software applications.
Embrace change and prioritize competing demands.
Comply with the Bank Secrecy Act (BSA) and other compliance requests and requirements.
Follow all safety and security guidelines to properly safeguard member and organization assets.
Perform other job related duties as assigned.
