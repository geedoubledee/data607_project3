The Health Equity team is dedicated to ensuring that all consumers and communities have equal opportunity to achieve their optimal health status.
In this role, you will use your data visualization skills to create impactful graphs, figures and reports that illuminate opportunities to address existing health disparities that disproportionally impact people of color, sexual and gender minorities, people with disabilities and other vulnerable populations.

You will work closely with a lead analytic consultant to identify and calculate relevant metrics, create appropriate data summaries, design and create dashboard products.
Key responsibilities include:
Develop and maintain consistent template and style guide for Health Equity dashboards
Design visually impactful, meaningful and easy to navigate dashboards that tell a story without overwhelming the end user with too much information
Own the end-to-end development process for new dashboards, from initial design through testing, revision, publication, automation and maintenance
Develop and validate metrics and calculations that are relevant and accurate
Present dashboards products to a wide variety of technical and non-technical audiences
Execute iterative updates to dashboards and coordinate releases of refreshed data and design enhancements
Maximize dashboard performance and troubleshoot technical issues
Manage dashboard access
