Job Description:
Fidelity is actively seeking an experienced solutions architect to work in the Asset Management Technology group. At Fidelity architects are leaders in technology playing a significant role in driving all aspects of technology planning and execution.
The Expertise and Skills You Bring
Experience with sophisticated event processing, RESTful API design, distributed architecture patterns and highly transaction trading integrations is required.
Experience in working with complex product and models for portfolio construction will be an added advantage.
Hands on experience in cloud technologies and practices, building data applications that use services such as AWS EC2, S3, SNS/SQS, API Gateway, AWS Batch, Lambda, Elasticsearch desired
Well-grounded knowledge of engineering and continuous delivery practices.
Proven leadership skills, demonstrated ability to mentor, influence and partner with application architects, engineering and product teams to deliver scalable robust data solutions.
Significant experience working in complex data environments in an engineering, design and architecture capacity.
Proficiency at understanding enterprise architecture concepts, master data management, data modeling and design techniques.
Strong skills and demonstrated focus in logical and component architecture design and deployment.
Demonstrated success in composing and driving long term product strategies
Proven knowledge and experience in Java, J2EE, RESTful web services, Apache Kafka, Gemfire and/or Akka platforms.
Strong experience in event based and message based orchestration architecture.
Passionate about technology and delivering solutions in integrated data environments.
Exposure to Python and R, along with Data Science and Machine Learning experience desired.
Detailed understanding of Data Exploration, Visual Analytics concepts and tools such as Tableau.
Hands on experience Amazon web services is desired.
Undergraduate degree in computer science or a related field ideally combined with an advanced technical degree.
Strong solution architecture, engineering and design skills.
Passion and curiosity, work with others to foster ideas and produce creative solutions to difficult problems while making smart choices balancing design and delivery.
A strong influencer who can integrate effectively into the culture. Combine facilitation skills with a results orientation to deliver excellence and execution.
Organizational savvy and maturity, work effectively across different organizational units and business groups.
Excellent interpersonal and communication skills with the ability to lead cross-team collaboration and partnerships across a variety of internal and external constituencies.
