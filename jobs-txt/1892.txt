Business Analyst I/II
Full-Time Salaried
Chicago, IL, US

Position Summary:
We are seeking a Business Analyst to provide analysis support for software development projects for various business areas globally including Reporting, Trading, Operations, Risk and Compliance. The job will include analysis of business to facilitate the drafting of functional specifications. The role will require communication with business managers and system users, internal development teams and external vendors.

Duties & Responsibilities

Responsibilities include but are not limited to:
Conduct interviews with project requestor and other stakeholders to fully understand project requirements
Gather, analyze, document, and review project proposals and business requirements with business managers and staff
Create written functional specifications and review business requirements and specifications with development teams.
Author written examples of business flows or transactions for complex requirements
Scope projects, create project plans and work with developers to create work estimates and schedules
Review and obtain requirements signoff from all stakeholders
Organize meetings to track project status, manage deadlines and work with IT to meet requirements and to anticipate ways to eliminate any obstacles
Assist in development of testing plans and scripts
Help, plan and participate in system testing and facilitate UAT
Qualifications / Skills Required:
Candidate should have a demonstrated aptitude and interest in technology and business, strong problem-solving skills and excellent communication skills.
Bachelor’s Degree - Information Systems (IS) or software related technology is preferred
Exceptional written and verbal skills
Ability to analyze and document current state processes and future state requirements
Ability to translate business requirements to a clear and logical functional specification that is coherent to IT developers and QA groups
Familiarity with key concepts of the business analyst roles in an organization and standard methods employed by business analysts
A can do attitude and strong work ethic
Skills Desired:
Ideally, the candidate should have a general sense of the trading, clearing cycle, risk and regulatory backdrop in the brokerage industry
A general understanding of database technologies
Good interpersonal skills with a focus on internal client service
Able to produce clear system process and workflow diagrams
Familiarity of various system development life cycle methodologies
Self-starter who can work independently or in a team
