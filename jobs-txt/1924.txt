Two Six Technologies is seeking an Associate Research Director-AI/ML/Data Science

Information and data are increasingly fundamental to new and emerging National Security capabilities being researched, developed, tested and deployed by the Department of Defense, the Intelligence Community and other National Security organizations. Two Six Technologies is seeking an AI/ML/Data Science leader to continue to build and deploy our capabilities to a wide range of mission partners, grow our sponsored research portfolio with DARPA and other research organizations, and cultivate our talented Data Scientists and AI/ML research team.
Responsibilities:
Set Technical Direction for AI/ML/Data Science R&D project portfolio
Cultivating relationships with DARPA Program Managers and other customers
Identifying new R&D opportunities and overseeing capture
Cultivating our AI/ML/Data Science talent team, identifying/hiring new team members and line management of key contributors
Technical marketing of Two Six Technologies' AI/ML/Data Science services and capabilities to new and existing Government clients
Requirements:
A four-year degree in Mathematics, Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or related technical domain
Ten or more years of professional experience in R&D Leadership
Past performance as a Principal Investigator on fast-paced, innovation-driven, advanced technology R&D projects for Government clients
Proven track record of capturing Government R&D contracts/programs
DoD and/or IC domain experience and knowledge
Nice to have:
An advanced degree (Master's or PhD) in Mathematics, Computer Science, Electrical Engineering or related technical domain
Experience as a Principal Investigator for a DARPA program
Experience serving as a line manager for a team of scientists and engineers
Academic and/or industry publications in relevant fields
Experience with agile software development practices
Experience developing AI/ML solutions and products
Experience leading teams in a rapid prototype environment demonstrating scalable architectures
Proven experience leading a rapidly growing team
An active security clearance
