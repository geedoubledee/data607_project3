Our Company
AICrete is a venture-backed startup focused on unlocking automation and machine learning in the concrete industry. Our innovative "concrete mixture and testing-as-a-service" model combines data, automation, and cutting-edge machine learning with a firm understanding of concrete materials science to enable concrete producers and contractors to make cost-effective, durable, and sustainable concrete mixes quickly, efficiently, and with minimal risk. Our mission is to make concrete mixture design and testing fast, cheap, and sustainable.

To do this job successfully, you need exceptional skills in statistics and programming. If you also have knowledge of data science, data engineering, and software engineering, we'd like to meet you. Your ultimate goal will be to shape and build efficient self-learning applications.
