Description
Reviews, analyzes, and evaluates process performance, driving audits and communicating out performance and next steps with the broader team. Relies on instructions and pre-established guidelines to perform the functions of the job. Works under immediate supervision. Primary job functions do not typically require exercising independent judgment

Responsibilities
They start with a morning standup to talk about what is happening in their world.
They will work closely with selection program manager on what needs to be tackled and will be required to join several meetings to sit in & learn more about the need.
Executing analysis (excel) and following up with stakeholder teams in regard to findings and action items coming out of the analysis.
Looking for someone who has between 1-2 years of business analyst experience. Supported by both the HM & direct reports. They want a self-starter and would like to see someone who has worked in an office job and is comfortable with excel and numbers.
Microsoft Excel, comfort with SQL, data/numbers, data analysis and reporting.
