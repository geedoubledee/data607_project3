As a Data Science Manager, you will lead a team of high-performing Data Scientists to enable the organization in delivering high value internal enterprise analytics use cases, as well as external enterprise analytics products for our customers.

Key responsibilities include:
Collaborate and influence team resourcing including engineering, data engineering, customer engagement, and product managers to ensure successful execution
Assist Engagement Leaders in the identification and prioritization process of analytics the team will take on by drawing on your breadth of previous development experiences
Utilize your deep technical expertise to influence direction for analytic solution development and assess performance thereof
Champion professional development of the Data Science team including through a formal development plan by identifying training needs and mentorship
Expand our thriving Analytics Community of Practice; Our organization benefits from talking to teams effectively, manage stakeholders, and executive level on the progress in projects in terms of partnerships; Leverage this network for the benefit of the team
Collaborate with Product Management, Program Management, Solution Architects, Customer Marketing, and R&D/Engineering/Development; Consistent with Agile, these teams are responsible for all the aspects of an offering, Discovery, Creation and Delivery
Keeping up to date on industry Developments and emerging technologies; Define next steps for HCE Analytics organization; Leverage new technologies to augment/replace pieces of our analytics platform
