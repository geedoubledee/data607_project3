Business Analyst
Position Summary
The Business Analyst is responsible for defining and executing strategies using technical solutions to address the client’s market challenges. If you enjoy tackling large scale challenges and then recommending and implementing solutions, this position is for you!

Duties and Responsibilities:
Convert business requirements into functional solution requirements that are utilized by software development resources
These requirements should be matched to the originating business requirement documents that drive the design
Document and perform end to end test cases that are supported by underlying user stories
Perform business analysis, identify root cause and develop recommendations/solutions to drive business improvements for the assets they represent
Analyze test defects and corrective actions for such defects and track to resolution and end user validation
Work with client solution architects, process engineers, UX/UI designer partners and developers to research components of solutions to problems
Apply policy and make decisions for defect vs enhancement
Help with data visualization, user experience and configuration elements of solution design
Work closely with Deployment Managers to align to release notes, training content and user guides with solution releases/implementation
Lead Joint Analysis and Design sessions for multiple assets and scope as assigned
Perform other duties as needed

Job Requirements
Education and Experience: The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required.
Master’s degree in Business Administration, Business Analytics, Computer Science, Systems Engineering or related field
5+ years of experience in an analyst role in the tech industry; preferably in software development
Experience using an issue/defect tracking tool such as Jira
Experience using a test management tool such as TestRail

Qualifications:
Working knowledge of IT industry
Strong verbal and written communication skills; excellent email, telephone and virtual meeting etiquette
Customer service orientated, ensuring needs are met in a timely fashion
Ability to talk through a data problem and come up with a way to resolve challenges
Ability to quickly analyze large data sets and draw inferences
Understanding of marketing analytics strategies and tools, including those necessary to formulate metrics for measuring brand asset valuation, strategies for performing marketing experiments, determining Customer Lifetime Value, assessment of cost of customer acquisition etc.
Ability to take opportunities for continuous improvement and implement documented process improvements
Ability to work on multiple projects concurrently; demonstrated organization, problem-solving, project management, and time management skills
Demonstrated strong ability to remotely work well within a team environment, or independently; strong interpersonal and teamwork skills; ability to work and interact effectively with team members from other disciplines, cultures, and organizations
Excellent verbal and written communication skills; ability to effectively inform and present ideas confidently and persuasively to peers and managers
Ability to take initiative to ask questions, ask for help, or provide updates, without reluctance
Ability to meet the eligibility requirements for working in the US without RLA sponsorship for employment visa status
Strong computer skills that include proficiency with MS Office (Word, Outlook, Excel, PowerPoint, and Visio), Teams and SharePoint
Language Skills
Ability to read, write, and speak English proficiently
