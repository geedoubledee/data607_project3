IT Business Analyst Job Description
The IT business analyst works with agency business units to assess processes, determine requirements, and deliver recommendations and reports to department stakeholders. This position must understand how changes to process, products, services, software, and hardware can improve efficiencies and add value while balancing them with what is technically feasible and financially and functionally reasonable. This position is responsible for representing the requirements of the agency and confirming they are met.
This IT business analyst will work on small, internally-developed IT software projects. Duties include:
Gathering business requirements from subject matter experts
Mapping business processes
Creating functional specifications for the solution, including user interfaces, external interfaces, interfaces, and reporting.
Creating high-level test plans for the solution
Ensuring that the business requirements are met in the final solution.
Working cooperatively with contractors, staff, and other agency representatives to resolve problems/issues.
The job may include evaluating software solutions for the agency to purchase.
