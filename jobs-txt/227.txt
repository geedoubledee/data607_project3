
Job Description
The main objective of this consultancy is to help compile data and create tools for processing stillbirth data and data quality assessment in the new area of work of UNICEF by producing reproducible codes, standard tools and templates that can be used for future stillbirth work. The consultant will assist with codes to update and optimize the stillbirth database and support developing tools for data quality assessments to under the supervision of the Statistics Specialist in the Data and Analytics Section, Division of Data, Research and Policy.
Timeframe: Start date:  March 1, 2019                 End date: August 31, 2019
