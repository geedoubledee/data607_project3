PolyCon Solutions is seeking a Mid-level Business Intelligence Developer to support our Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) client with the analysis and evaluation of user business problems and the development of system recommendations to meet requirements. The ideal candidate analyzes current and technical processes to formulate and develop new and modified business information processing systems. They also will coordinate with business and technology teams to ascertain system requirements, such as program functions, output requirements, input data acquisition, execution of user acceptance, and may coordinate testing and system controls. This position requires a great deal of interactions with the FAA Security Team, FAA executives and individual team members.
Responsibilities:
Helps to mature Power BI reporting
Leads the development of operational dashboards, periodic business reviews, and effective metrics
Analyzes complex business problems and issues using data to provide insight to decision makers
Participates in cross-functional teams to address business issues
Works closely with Business Analysts and end users to evaluate business requirements and advise on appropriate informative views and dashboards
Designs, develops, deploys, monitors, reports and improves enterprise process data visualization
Develops Power BI reports, dashboards, publish reports to power BI service and establish gateway refresh schedules using DAX and PowerPivot BI
Works on Microsoft BI analytics stack (SSIS, SSAS) and work on investigating new tools, tool capabilities and engage with Business to propose, manage, and implement solutions
Develops SSAS tabular models utilizing complex DAX functions and SQL queries, stored procedures using T-SQL, CTE, XMLA, PowerShell scripts
Works on complex data modelling and design patterns for BI/Analytics reporting requirements
Develops proof of concepts to mock up complex reporting requirements with upcoming technologies on Microsoft Analytics platform
Maintains, updates, and manages audit reports, and oversee data error corrections with team managers
Required Qualifications:
Proven SQL & Query writing skills
Significant Power Platform (Power BI, Power Apps, Power Automate) experience
Hands-on knowledge of Oracle and SQL Server SQL, as well as strong understanding of relational and dimensional data modeling
Ability to document business requirements and translate into technical reporting specifications
Able to obtain a Public Trust clearance
Self-starter with the ability to work independently, while at the same time being a solid group contributor with strong leadership skills
Education and Experience:
Bachelor's Degree in Data Analytics, Systems Engineering or a related field with 8 years of relative experience
