The Business Analyst position will support the Volpe National Transportation Systems Center managing complex, high-profile, and exciting programs and projects. Support will require business analysis skills, focused on planning, to ensure product/project goals, functionality, and customer specifications and expectations are met and managed; as well as ensure that processes are properly documented. Additionally, the Business Analyst will be the liaison between the client and technical teams and drive innovative solutions, leveraging best practices and practical business analysis principles.
Duties will include:
Collecting and analyzing project business requirements, documenting them to define and inform the project team and/or client
Evaluating data collected through task analysis, business process improvement, interviews, surveys, and meetings
Preparing accurate and detailed documentation, including project plans, requirement specifications, user interface guides, job aids, and functional specification documents
Communicating effectively with internal teams and external clients to assist in the delivery of project deliverables, including functional requirements
Effectively documenting the acquired results of analysis and workflows, with an emphasis on data visualization tools and techniques
Validating data and document and report discrepancies
Conducting quality assurance analyses on documented processes and their features and functionality
Managing change requests related to project plans to meet agreed upon deadlines
Experience with building test case scenarios using an Automated Testing tool (Selenium or similar)
Experience with tools used to help teams plan, assign, track, report and manage work (JIRA or similar)
Requirements:
Bachelor's Degree or Master's Degrees in Business Administration, Computer Science, Economics, or any related field from an accredited institution with 5+ years of relevant work experience
Ability to work independently with minimal supervision (self-starter) and or with a team for a common goal
Excellent written and oral communication and presentation skills, as well as experience with strategic planning and project planning and implementation
Ability to think both abstractly and analytically, with an emphasis on problem-solving
Ability to gather and interpret relevant data and information from a variety of sources, often requiring research skills and creativity
Proficient skills in MS Word, Outlook, PowerPoint, Excel & SQL
Ability to work calmly and constructively under pressure and meet deadlines
Ability to manage and keep track of multiple tasks simultaneously
Must have good attention to detail and be well-organized
Demonstrated trustworthiness
