Job Title: Business Intelligence Developer
Job Location: 100% Remote – East Coast hours
Job Duration: Long-Term
Job Description:
The Executive Office of Technology Services and Security (EOTSS) is seeking an experienced Business Intelligence (BI) Developer to join our Data Office.
As part of the Commonwealth’s central data team, you’ll be on the front lines working to improve the value of our data systems across state government.
As a BI developer, you will work in a vital role to deliver policy and operational insights to our partner agencies and improve the Commonwealth’s capacity for data-driven decision-making.
Responsibilities:
Translate business requirements into technical specifications
Access, transform, and format data to meet business needs
Design, build, deploy, and maintain BI solutions that meet high standards for functionality, usability, accessibility, and visual appeal in Tableau, PowerBI, and other BI tools
Conduct user acceptance testing and troubleshoot issues
Develop and update technical documentation
Support and assist state agency partners with BI skill-building
Knowledge, skills & abilities:
At least three (3) years of proven, professional experience building effective BI solutions that meet business needs
Working expertise in common BI tools, including Tableau and PowerBI
Strong analytics skills
Strong UX/design skills; ability to create intuitive, aesthetic data visualizations that will be useful and used
In-depth understanding of database management systems, online analytical processing, and ETL frameworks
Proficiency in SQL
Able to keep pace with project timelines, including periods of fast-paced, sequential deadlines
Experience in an Agile work environment
Experience with government administrative data a plus
Candidates selected for interviews should be prepared to present examples of their work.
Education and Certifications:
Bachelor’s Degree in computer science, information systems, business administration, data science or related field, or equivalent work experience
