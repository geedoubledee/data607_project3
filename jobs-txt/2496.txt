Reports to: CP Head of Marketing
The Role: Director of Strategy, Data & Insights
The Director of Strategy, Data and Insights will be responsible for gathering, interpreting and synthesizing and distributing insights and analysis to advise the consumer business unit on best-in-class marketing strategies that drive growth. This proactive and shopper-centric leader will be responsible for sharing unique LTK insights across the CP Marketing team, making strategic investment recommendations to grow daily active users (DAUs), and will oversee the implementation of media MarTech to drive and measure conversion.
Key Responsibilities and Accountabilities:
Establish quarterly goals, KPIs and OKRs for CP teams using historical data and predictive analytics; with a focus on paid media measurement and organic channel measurement/halo-impact.
Partner with CP Marketing leaders and SMEs, to identify short- and long-term strategic plans and recommendations to drive DAUs, GMV and/or offset risk.
Lead the selection and implementation of media MarTech to support consumer and creator investment initiatives; including segmentation tools, ad-servers, and measurement platforms.
Effectively make a prediction, provide it out with data, and tell a cohesive story with insights the company can use.
Develop analysis from internal and external data sources, develop competitive benchmarking, and identify trends and disruptive market shifts that support user growth by way of Creators.
Consider and analyze strategic questions that are core to our business.
Engage with senior leadership on business challenges and present frameworks, insights and findings to help the company move forward confidently.
Drive innovation in dashboard offerings, partnering with the Consumer Insights team.
Operate with a deep, empathetic understanding of our customers.
Champion team development and expansion across the organization.
Partner with stakeholders to pilot new initiatives/opportunities, measure results, and optimize outcomes.
Participate as an active member of the extended leadership team by communicating challenges, opportunities and proposed solutions.
