WHAT YOU’LL DO

As a senior member of the growing Data Science team in Bain’s Advanced Analytics Group, you will:
Work with Global AAG leadership and local Mexico City Partners to build out our Advanced Analytics capabilities in Mexico
Collaborate closely with and influence business consulting staff and leaders as part of multi-disciplinary teams to assess opportunities and develop data driven solutions for Bain clients across a variety of sectors
Translate business objectives into data and analytics solutions and, translate results into business insights using appropriate data engineering and data science applications
Partner closely with Engineering and product leaders at Bain to support development of innovative analytics solutions and products
Provide thought leadership in state-of-the-art machine learning techniques
Develop and deploy industry-leading machine learning models, aimed at solving client problems across industry verticals and business functions.
Help develop Advanced Analytics intellectual property and identify areas of new opportunity for data science and analytics for Bain and its clients
This position will be located in one of the US Bain offices and will require regular travel to Mexico City
Travel is required
Lead recruiting and onboarding for local data science talent
Ensure standards and guidelines are being used by team to develop re-usable common frameworks, model and components that address repeatable tasks and problem/data sets in specific industries or business functions
Share learnings and drive adoption of best demonstrated practices with your teammates in AAG about theoretical and technical developments in the rapidly evolving data science field, collaborate with the open source community, and work together to create a great working environment that attracts other great data scientists
Develop relationships with external data and analytics vendors and interact as needed
Keep abreast of new and current data science techniques and technologies
Provide client and client development support
Participate in expert advisory activities that required deep expertise in data science domain
Advise client executives on data and analytics strategy and roadmap design
Work with consulting teams to understand business problems, translate them into data science projects and appropriately prioritize and execute
Be an expert advisor in proposal discussions
Act as an ambassador and coach towards data science teams at our clients and partners to raise their capabilities and ensure that our work is successfully deployed
Other duties as required
ABOUT YOU

We are looking for someone who has:
Advanced Degree in a quantitative discipline such as Computer Science, Engineering, Econometrics, Statistics or Information Sciences such as business analytics or informatics
10+ years of experience in analytics
3+ years of experience managing data scientists
Shipped production, enterprise scale data products
Track record of leading and collaborating on strategic initiatives
Strategic consulting experience is a plus
Strong interpersonal and communication skills, including the ability to explain and discuss mathematical and machine learning technicalities with colleagues and clients from other disciplines
Interest in learning about business applications, business models and economics of machine learning and data technologies - you will have the opportunity to work with world-leading practitioners in this area
Ability to collaborate with people at all levels and with multi-office/region teams
Solid fundamental knowledge of supervised, unsupervised, reinforcement learning machine learning algorithms such as classifiers, cluster analysis, dimension reduction, regression, CNN, RNN, DQN, GAN, temporal difference methods, sequence modeling, NLP/NLU, word/doc embeddings, collaborative filtering, self-attention, transformers, etc.
Experience with MLOps - scalable development to deployment of complex data science workflows
Experience with model explainability and interpretability techniques

Experience with cloud platforms required
Experience in big data technologies such as Hadoop, BigQuery, S3, MapReduce, Apache Spark is required
Ability to work independently and juggle priorities to thrive in a fast paced environment, while also collaborating as part of a team in complex situations
Fluency in Spanish and English
