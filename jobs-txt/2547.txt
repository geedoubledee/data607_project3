Position Summary:
The Business Analyst reviews, analyzes, and evaluates user needs to create solutions that support overall business strategies. Documents system requirements, defines scope and objectives, and assists in the creation of specifications that drive development and implementation. Incumbents function as a liaison between development and users.
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
Break down written specifications by vendors into actionable items by development teams.
Clearly communicate and document those business requirements into product backlog items.
Participate in ongoing requirement grooming working with both internal and external sources.
Participate in the agile development process by attending stand ups, sprint planning, and other activities as needed.
Participate in functional quality assurance as needed to help verify newly developed items meet business requirements.
