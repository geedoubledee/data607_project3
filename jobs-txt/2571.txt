Savan Group is actively recruiting for Director of Data Solutions to serve in a senior management and practice lead role while providing subject matter expertise and thought leadership in data strategy and data science, including but not limited to data strategy, management, governance, analytics, artificial intelligence/natural language processing, and visualization. This is a strategic role within our organization, and you will be responsible for maturing our services and capabilities across projects and defining our service offerings for our clients. Specifically, responsibilities include the following:
Roles and Responsibilities
Service Delivery
Be able to effectively shape, plan, and help management staff engagements
Provide subject matter expertise and thought leadership to projects
Take responsibility for helping project teams develop solutions and services consistent with the data strategy and data science service area
Have a strong understanding of risk management and how to protect the interests of both the client and Savan Group
Understand how to build teams of capability leads and establish the right mix of people and capabilities to serve the client best and strengthen the relationship
Have a broader perspective of potential issues encountered when managing diverse teams and the strategies to overcome them; have a clear understanding of the firm’s commitment to creating a more inclusive culture
Maintain minimum billable target of 50% on existing projects
Business Development
Assist in the development of content to build capability awareness and brand on website and social media
Promote and facilitate collaboration and standardization of mission support services projects across verticals
Build, maintain, and adapt service offerings to client environments and needs
Research and develop technology offerings to advance capabilities
Manage and grow pipeline of business development opportunities
Lead white paper and proposal development for related opportunities
Identify and pursue key partnerships
Possess a strong commercial acumen and be able to demonstrate a track record of creating, selling, and delivering large-scale engagements
Develop a personal contact network across Savan Group and in the federal marketplace
Quality, Innovation and Brand
Support the continued growth of the business through contributing to the wider development of the data strategy and data science practice (e.g., supporting the development of proposals, whitepapers, training, marketing, communications, and recruitment activities)
Lead or play a key role in coordinating service area maturation (e.g., Training, Marketing, Communications or Recruitment)
Human Resources
Take a selfless interest in the development, success (and happiness) of Savan Group staff, especially those working for you
Support the career development of others by acting as a mentor or counselor, consulting staff
Create position descriptions, lead interviews and assist in the recruiting and creation of a pipeline of data strategy and data science capability candidates in concert with Vertical Leads and Project Managers.
Preferred Qualifications and Requirements
Bachelor’s degree in computer science, information systems, or related degree
U.S. Citizenship
At least 15 years of professional experience
5+ years of government consulting experience
Possess a U.S. Public Trust or ability to obtain a Public Trust
Highly technical subject matter expertise with data strategy, governance, management, visualization, and analytics
Strong knowledge of data storage, metadata, tagging, and data warehousing
Strong knowledge of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and natural language processing to create value from structured and unstructured data
Possess a growth-oriented mindset and a passion for learning new things
Job Location and Schedule
Savan Group Headquarters (McLean, VA)
Hybrid Schedule
