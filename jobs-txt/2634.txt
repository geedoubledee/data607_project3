Overview:
BOOST LLC is a dynamic management consulting firm that offers an array of government-compliant back-office solutions to support our teaming partners within the GovCon space. Our consultants are experts in the areas of Accounting, Contracts, Human Resources, Recruiting & Sourcing, and Strategic Pricing and our passion is to guide and propel our partners towards success within this competitive sector.

BOOST is helping one of our teaming partners hire a well-qualified Director of Data Solutions to join their team and serve in a senior management and practice lead role. Our client is an innovative management and information technology consultancy with a relentless commitment to delivering value and driving the entrepreneurial spirit.

You will be responsible for delivering subject matter expertise and thought leadership in data strategy and data science, including but not limited to data strategy, management, governance, analytics, artificial intelligence/natural language processing, and visualization. This is a strategic role and you will be responsible for maturing our services and capabilities across projects and defining our service offerings for our clients. This is a full-time position with a hybrid schedule out of McLean, VA.
