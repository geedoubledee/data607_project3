
About Caption HealthCaption Health’s mission is to detect disease early – when there is the highest potential for impact – by leveraging artificial intelligence and ultrasound. Our breakthrough AI platform enables any healthcare professional to perform high-quality ultrasound exams for early disease detection, in convenient and lower-cost outpatient settings including patients’ homes. It was recognized as one of TIME’s 100 Best Inventions of 2021 and one of Fast Company’s Next Big Things in Health Tech. Through our work with health plans, providers, patients, and industry partners, we are transforming care, expanding access, and reducing costs. To learn more, visit captionhealth.com.We are seeking an experienced Machine Learning Engineer to join our team and make an impact!
