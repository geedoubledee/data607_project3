
Company DescriptionForth Point is a Data Science and Data Engineering company headquartered in Edinburgh UK with plans to grow a large team across EMEA in the next 5 years. The core aim of the business is to create the largest UK independent data and analytics specialist focused on delivering against the promise of the 4th industrial revolution. We believe that data that doesn't drive value is lost opportunity, and we are passionate about helping our clients drive better outcome through applied analytics.  The promise of the industry 4.0 is that connectivity between devices has the potential to deliver huge gains in productivity, efficiency, and innovation but without harnessing the power of this interconnected world, that promise will never be realised.  We are obsessed with delivering world class solutions to our customers through our network of industry leading partners.
