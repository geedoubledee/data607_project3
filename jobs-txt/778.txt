Welcome to Bosch.The Robert Bosch GmbH is looking forward to your application!Job DescriptionAs a data scientist, you will be responsible for the concept and the development of a machine learning platform where you take over the responsibility for the overall architecture as well as its further development with a special focus on customer requirements as well as reusability and state-of-the-art technologies (FaaS, SaaS).You create solutions in customer projects for complex issues in MEMS development (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems development) using ML (Machine Learning) methods, including requirements analysis, planning of work packages and documentation.Also, you will lead coordination efforts to create complex data models of different systems (hardware, software, lab, production) and be able to inspire experienced developers of new technologies.Furthermore, you can significantly shape the use of cross-functional data engineering methods (Spark) for measurement and manufacturing data with a focus on MEMS.Your area of responsibility includes the coordination and clarification of requirements with internal and external partners. In addition, you will have the opportunity to actively shape and monitor the current state of research through collaboration with universities and research groups.QualificationsEducation: completed university studies (Master/Diploma/PhD) in electrical engineering, computer science, mathematics, physics or a comparable subjectExperience and Knowledge: several years of professional experience in the field of data science combined with data architectures using machine learning methods on Hadoop and with Spark and Apache Airflow;
