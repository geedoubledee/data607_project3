
What   You'll DoThis position is based in Washington DC at the United States Agency for International Development’s (USAID) Office of Budget and Resource Management (BRM). BRM works with USAID operating units in Washington, D.C and the field to optimize the strategic planning and allocations of USAID’s Foreign Assistance Budget. In addition to serving the analytical needs of the Agency, BRM collaborates with interagency, international development organizations, and public partners in the development community. The Mid-Level Data Scientist is responsible for working with subject matter experts to address and enhance the Agency’s capacity to integrate data and analysis into routine decision making. They will apply analytical methodologies to diagnose data-related challenges, implement solutions, and evaluate performance. They will perform data integration, data visualization, data mining, and analysis tools to arrive at results
Technical Support and Analytical Services
